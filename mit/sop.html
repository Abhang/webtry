I wonder what is the meaning of being religious? I always thought it is about having peace within, and looking at fellow beings with love. It also means that one is just in day to day lives and bigger topics that matter. However, I realized that it is not the case. Most people are religious for a sense of community, for a sense of belonging to part of a larger group, and it has nothing to do with the teachings of religion. Though they pray and say good stuff as part of it, and they know the deeper meanings of the religious texts, but unfortunately they dont practice what they preach.

What is the use of practicing religion this way? How does it help make the world a better place?

This is contradictory to the belif that only riteous people are proctected, however contradiction is unbeknowest to them as they truely believe they are riteous, when they are not!
